MergiCal

MergiCal merges your iCalendar feeds so that your relations know when you are busy.

  • Merge your work, friends, sports, etc. calendars into a single event feed.
  • Generate customized feeds containing only a subset of your calendars.
  • Set the visibility of each calendar to show the events full details or only your availability.

Magical? No. Just MergiCal. Sign in with your Google account.
